Signs You Need HVAC Repair

Here are the top signs your system needs professional attention:

  • Unusual Noises: Banging, clanking, or grinding noises indicate mechanical issues.
  • Short Cycling: If your system keeps turning off and on, it may be struggling to maintain temperature.
  • Inconsistent Temperatures: If some rooms are warmer or colder than others, airflow might be restricted.
  • Foul Odors: Musty or burning smells are warning signs of mold or electrical problems.
